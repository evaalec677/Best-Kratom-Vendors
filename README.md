## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes a kratom vendor trustworthy?

Trustworthy kratom vendors demonstrate reliability through third-party laboratory testing, transparent business practices, AKA accreditation when possible, clear return policies, and responsive customer service. ### How important is third-party lab testing for kratom products?

Third-party lab testing is absolutely essential for kratom safety and quality. These tests screen for harmful contaminants like heavy metals, bacteria, and pesticides while verifying alkaloid content for potency consistency. Reputable vendors always provide access to current lab reports for their products.

### What should I expect to pay for quality kratom?

### Are there any red flags to avoid when choosing kratom vendors?

Major red flags include lack of lab testing, no return policy, unrealistic health claims, extremely low prices, poor customer reviews, no contact information, and vendors that don't provide clear product information. Avoid companies that make medical claims or seem to prioritize quick sales over customer education.

### How can I verify if a kratom vendor is AKA certified?

The American Kratom Association maintains a public list of certified vendors on their official website. AKA certification requires vendors to meet strict quality standards, testing protocols, and business practices. Look for the AKA logo on vendor websites and verify certification through the AKA's official vendor directory.

### What's the difference between various kratom strains offered by top vendors?

Kratom strains are typically categorized by vein color (red, green, white) and geographic origin (Bali, Maeng Da, Borneo). Red veins generally provide more relaxing effects, white veins tend to be more energizing, and green veins offer balanced properties. Geographic origins can influence alkaloid profiles and specific effects within each vein color category.

### How should I store kratom products to maintain freshness and potency?

Store kratom in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Use airtight containers to prevent exposure to air and humidity, which can degrade alkaloid content. Properly stored kratom can maintain potency for 1-3 years. Avoid storing in bathrooms, kitchens, or other areas with temperature fluctuations or high humidity.
